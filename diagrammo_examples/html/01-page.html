The Pirate Code

Articles of Agreement, Nassau 1716

Article I — Loot Division

  • Captain receives two shares
  • Quartermaster receives one and a half shares
  • All other crew receive one share each

Article II — Conduct

  • Every crew member has a vote in affairs of the moment
  • Lights out by eight — drinking after hours on the open deck only
  • Keep your weapons clean and ready for action at all times
“The Code is more what you’d call guidelines than actual rules.”
Signed in blood at Nassau, Year of Our Lord 1716
